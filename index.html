Underwater Torpedo League

The Underwater Torpedo League is a game played entirely underwater. The objective of the game is to deposit the torpedo in the opposing teams goal without committing a foul. Each game consists of three matches. Each match is played with a point scoring round that is played to five points per match. The team that reaches five points first wins the match. The team that wins two of the three matches wins the game.
